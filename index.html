Bar Queue App

The app it directed to the viewpoint of the outgoers that like to go out on days where bars or restaurants are giving specials on that day, or for those that like to sit down and enjoy their meal with other associates but donâ€™t want to have to wait very long to be able to sit. It also gives the perspective of those that want to go and have a drink or go to a night event that may have a big crowd and admission fee.

Importance

This solution is needed due to the aspects that lines can be long for specific bars or restaurants. Also you would not want to be blindsided by admission fees or you arrive to the location of your desire just to find out that the establishment is closed.

The purpose of this app is to provide more accuracy with ease to specific bars or restaurants that are in the surrounding area in the categories of admission fees, popularity, wait times, gender ratio, open/close times, and possible specials that are being given by each establishment.

Goal Functionality

Google Maps with filtered results for Bars and Restaurants. Users Check In to update current admission fees and wait times. Color coded map icons for wait times. Owners given the ability to update specials, events, and hours of operation.

Possible Advancements

Owner verification through Google's Mail Postcards System. Owners avialibility to view demographics, historic wait times, and other analytics.

Future Developments

Push Notifications from specific bars or restaurants to Checked In users. Viewable reviews from popular review sites about individual bars or restaurants that are more recent (2012 and later).
